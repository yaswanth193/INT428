🔍 10-Point Order Verification

Complete all 10 checks to verify your delivered order is genuine and matches what you negotiated.

1
Package Seal Intact
Check that the original manufacturer seal has not been opened or tampered with.
2
Serial Number Match
Compare the serial number on the box with the one on the device or invoice.
3
Product Condition
Inspect for scratches, dents, broken parts, or signs of prior use.
4
All Accessories Present
Check charger, cable, manual, warranty card and any box inserts are included.
5
Invoice & Price Check
Confirm invoice shows the negotiated price — not the original MRP.
6
Correct Model/Variant
Verify storage size, colour, and model number exactly match your order.
7
Device Powers On
Switch the product on, confirm it boots normally and shows no error screen.
8
Warranty Registration
Confirm the warranty card is blank/unused and the device is still under active warranty.
9
Seller Authenticity
Cross-check seller name and rating on the platform matches the verified seller in your deal.
10
NegotiAI Deal Match
Confirm delivery matches the product, price, and seller locked by NegotiAI.
